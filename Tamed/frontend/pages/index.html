About TAMED

TAMED is a purpose-driven Cardano stake pool dedicated to transforming lives through education. We channel 100% of our profits into scholarships for underserved Kenyan students.

Staking with TAMED offers 2–6% rewards every 5 days with a low 170 ADA fee and 1% margin, maximizing returns while making a meaningful impact.

Our History, Vision & Mission

Our History

TAMED began in 2023 with Linkberry’s sponsorship, addressing Kenyan students’ educational challenges. We’ve grown into a thriving stake pool, funding laptops, tuition, YouTube channels, and AI training.

Our Vision

To create a world where every Kenyan student has access to quality education and opportunities via the Cardano blockchain, empowering future leaders.

Our Mission

To use ADA staking profits to fund scholarships and skill programs for Kenyan youth, ensuring a secure, low-cost pool that supports educational equity.

Our Cutting-Edge Technology

TAMED uses Azure cloud servers, redundant backup nodes, and offline key storage for 24/7 uptime, monitored by Prometheus and Grafana for top security.
